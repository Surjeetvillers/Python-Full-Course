Angela's Cinammon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • ¾ cup of milk
  • 2¼ of teaspoons yoguurt
  • ¼ Cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cups of brown sugar
  • 1½ Tablespoons of ground cinnammon
  • ¼ Cup butter
    •

    Instructions

    1. Mix the milk with yeast,Suagr,eggs
    2. Melt the bultter and add it to the mixture
    3. Add it into the flour and mix until combined into dough
    4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
    5. Tranfer the dough to the larger bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere for 2 hours to rise
    6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into larger rectangle.
    7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix it in the sugar and cinnammon
    8. Spread the filling onto the dough and then roll into swiss roll
    9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into the baking tray
    10. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C,then bake the rolls for 20 to 25mins until lightly brown
