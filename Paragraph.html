First, address your email to the proper person. Check the human resources directory to find the person assigned to handle the type of problem you need addressed. If you're concerned about your issue being taken seriously, you can also reach out directly to the head of human resources. Make sure to double-check that only the person you want to contact is addressed in the email.

Next, write a specific subject line showing that action is needed. A clear subject line communicating both your problem and the level of urgency you're assigning to it will help human resources prioritize your problem.
